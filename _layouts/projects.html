Projects

NBA Player Interaction Network Analysis

This was the final project for my Network Theory class. My partner and I chose to analyze the assist networks of all NBA players from 2001-2024, using data obtained with NBA API. These network visualizations can be filtered to specific teams, or all NBA players over a single year. Hovering a node shows player-specific statistics.

Zelp

A Yelp-inspired social web app for university students. This was a 10-week project for the San Diego Supercomputing Center summer 2024 internship. I collaborated with 5 other undergraduate UCSD students to learn about the Agile development process, working all over the front- and back-end.
